bagels

The café offers an array of bagels, freshly served every day, made with intricacy and care.

All are baked fresh and directly served from the open as the store is open.

We have an everything bagel sprinkle to go preferably with plain but can go with everything. Just when you need the classic.

plain bagel [P50]

As plain as it gets, 16g

- Texture is on point, has the right amount of crispiness

- Hardness is balanced; soft but it can still hold its shape

greens bagel [P60]

Made with spinach and zucchini, 19g

- Greens are well incorporated, leaving no hint of taste

- Small bits of zucchini are present

bagel w/ red berries [P60]

Made with safe, non-toxic red berries, 18g

- Berries are cooked, leaving a semi-crunchy feel when you eat it

- Red berry jam is used

choco bagel with chips [P65]

Dark chocolate, 17g

- Bitter nibs (can be replaced with sweeter chips) are present

- Bagel leans on the tasteless side, with a hint of sweetness

coffee bagel with coffee nibs [P65]

Made with coffee, 17g

- Coffee taste can range from bitter to sweet

- A bit moist, doesn't ruin the shape

earl grey bagel [P65]

Made with earl grey tea, 17g

- Citrusy flavor, not too strong

- Lemon zest present as you bit into it
